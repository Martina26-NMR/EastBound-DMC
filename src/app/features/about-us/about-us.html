CHANGING THE CONCEPT OF TRAVEL

From Destinations To Real Experiences

Tourism has always been about destinations, but we believe it should be about experiences.

Egypt and Jordan have long been marketed as ready-made destinations – visit the pyramids, swim in the Dead Sea, and that's it. Eastbound was founded to change that. We saw two of the world's most amazing countries reduced to mere stereotypes, and we knew there was so much more to discover.

From the vibrant streets of Cairo to the serene sunrise in Wadi Rum, from the hidden layers of Petra to the hustle and bustle of Khan El Khalili in the morning, the true magic of this region lies in the details. And we know where to find them.

We are a company with an ambitious mission: to become the pioneers of travel to Egypt and Jordan. And we're just getting started.
